### Analysis: sim_recorded data format
Investigated GR00T sim filter pipeline (batch_sim_record.py):
- **Not just filtering**: Full physics re-simulation through GEAR-SONIC policy in MuJoCo
- **sim_recorded/successful/*.npz** contains: `dof_pos(N,29)`, `dof_vel(N,29)`, `actions(N,29)`, `torques(N,29)`, `root_pos(N,3)`, `root_quat(N,4)`, `ref_frame(N,)`, `fps=50`
- **Key difference from GMR retarget**: sim_recorded is at 50Hz (not ~30Hz), has velocity/torque data, is physically validated
- **metadata.json** in GMR_retarget/ maps filenames to babel_sid for text annotation matching

### Sim filter pipeline analysis (GR00T-WholeBodyControl)
Full investigation of `GR00T-WholeBodyControl/sim_filter/batch_sim_record.py`:
- **Not just filtering**: Full physics re-simulation through GEAR-SONIC tracking policy in MuJoCo
- Pipeline: `GMR_retarget PKL (43-DOF, ~30Hz)` → `convert_gmr_pkl_to_sonic_npz.py` → `sonic_npz (29-DOF)` → `batch_sim_record.py + GEAR-SONIC` → `sim_recorded (50Hz, physically simulated)`
- sim_recorded npz keys: `dof_pos(N,29)`, `dof_vel(N,29)`, `actions(N,29)`, `torques(N,29)`, `root_pos(N,3)`, `root_quat(N,4)`, `ref_frame(N,)`, `fps=50`
- Fall detection: pelvis Z < 0.15m → marked as failed
- Conclusion: `sim_recorded/successful/` is re-generated data (not filtered copies), making upstream `GMR_retarget/` and `sonic_npz/` redundant
- Conclusion: `extract_dataset_g1.py` can be skipped since sim_recorded already has dof_pos/root_pos/root_quat
- Conclusion: `seq_data_g1/` and `mp_data_g1/` are stale (built from unfiltered 2660, not filtered 2282)
