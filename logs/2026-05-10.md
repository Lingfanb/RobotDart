<summary><h3 style="display:inline">🚦 架构决策:A 路线降级 → D 路线确立</h3></summary>

> **早上 user 提出**: 重新设计 MPC-style autoregressive diffusion + custom WBC + 物理约束 + VAD condition,5-7 周训练。
>
> **下午调研发现 BeyondMimic** (Berkeley/Stanford, arXiv 2508.08241, Aug 2025) **几乎就是 A 路线提案的 locomotion 版**:
>
> | A 路线提案元素 | BeyondMimic 已有 |
> |---|---|
> | MPC-aware autoregressive diffusion motion gen | guided-diffusion policy distill |
> | 物理约束 reward (anti-slip / balance) | DeepMimic-style + foot contact reward |
> | Test-time waypoint / yaw guidance | classifier guidance (waypoint / joystick / obstacle) |
> | 自训 RL WBC | PPO tracker + adaptive sampling |
> | 跟踪 own prediction (MPC 闭环) | tracker + diffusion 都是 receding-horizon |
> | G1 + sim2real | G1 + IsaacLab 2.1 + sim2real (姊妹 repo) |
>
> A 路线 (5-7 周自造) 写出 paper 站不住 — reviewer 会直接 "this is BeyondMimic"。**改 D 路线: fork + 加 anti-slip reward + 加 VAD classifier guidance**,2-3 周搞定,把 novelty 收紧到 VAD + cross-channel + user study(这是 BeyondMimic 没碰的)。
>
> Proposal v0.2 加了 D 路线 + framing section: [docs/notes/decisions/mpc_diffusion_wbc_redesign_proposal_2026-05-09.md](docs/notes/decisions/mpc_diffusion_wbc_redesign_proposal_2026-05-09.md)

<details open>
<summary><h3 style="display:inline">⚠️ VAD humanoid 竞争扫描(paper "first" 主张验证)</h3></summary>

> 调研 "VAD-conditioned humanoid motion gen / locomotion / manipulation" 已发表竞争者:
>
> **3 篇 2025-2026 papers 已经做了 V+A continuous on humanoid (gesture)** — "first humanoid VAD" claim **不成立**:
>
> | # | Paper | Robot | 条件 | 通道 | 用户研究 | 威胁度 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | 1 | HIAER (Bao, arXiv 2506.01563, Jun 2025) | G1 real | V+A continuous, no D | gesture | n/a | **need user 澄清**:这是 Lingfan 1 作?和 RAL V-A DDIM 是同一篇吗? |
> | 2 | RG-VLMD (arXiv 2603.18771, Mar 2026) | NAO real | V+A via VLMD | gesture | n/a | HIGH — 独立组 2 个月前 |
> | 3 | ReSIn-HR (arXiv 2601.17287, Jan 2026) | NAO real | V+A from speech | gesture | N=20, 7-pt Likert | HIGH — 已闭环 + 人测 |
> | 4 | Text2Gestures (arXiv 2101.11101, 2021) | 虚拟角色,**非 humanoid** | V+A+D full | body gesture | yes | medium — 唯一用 D 的但是 animation |
>
> **完全无人区**(我们 unique 落点):
> - VAD-conditioned humanoid **manipulation / handover** — 0 篇
> - **Cross-channel coherence**(同一 VAD 调 gesture + handover + locomotion)— 0 篇
> - **D 维度 + humanoid + 真机** — 0 篇
> - N≥30 affect user study + humanoid — 0 篇
>
> **NMI defensible framing**(load-bearing 关键词必须组合出现):`first + cross-channel + contact + V+A+D + N=30`。任何一个掉,first claim 破。
>
> Paper §2 / §6 必须加 3 句保护(主动 cite + 划清界线):
> 1. "Prior continuous V/A-conditioned humanoid motion generation [HIAER, RG-VLMD, ReSIn-HR] is **limited to upper-body co-speech gesture**; none address contact-mediated channels or unify expression across skill families."
> 2. "Prior V+A+D motion generation [Text2Gestures] is **restricted to virtual character animation**; no prior work closes the loop on a physical humanoid platform with a contact-rich task."
> 3. "We position our contribution against the in-lab RAL building block [Bao et al., RAL 2026] which established V+A on G1 gesture; the NMI extension adds **D**, **handover**, **closed-loop**, and **cross-channel** consistency."
