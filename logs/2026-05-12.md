**1. Regressor v1.3 final 锁定 (7 indicators)**

| 维 | 公式 | 权重 | v1.2 → v1.3 变化 |
|---|---|---|---|
| V | smoothness · body_contraction · **chest_height** | 0.40 / 0.35 / 0.25 | V3 改:sin(pitch) asymmetric → **shoulder z keypoint** (避免 V/D 共用 root_pitch 信号) |
| A | mean_speed · accel_peak | 0.60 / 0.40 | **去掉 jerk_l1** (V1 内部仍用),mean_speed 改 **peak-centered Gaussian** (Flash & Hogan 1985 minimum-jerk, σ=15 frames) |
| D | reach_extension · **forward_lean** | **0.40 / 0.60** ⭐ Option C | D2 改:translation → sin(pitch) signed(lean-dominant);D3 directness 移除;静态动作也能 score |

设计原则:V (shape) · A (kinematics) · D (outward action / lean toward target)。V-D 物理 channel disjoint (V 用 shoulder z + dof,D 用 root pitch + wrist x)。
