<summary><h2 style="display:inline">5/9 晚 — Hardware variance + reorg parity + latent FM PoC</h2></summary>

> 重构后 sanity 训练比 pre-reorg sf 差 -15%, 怀疑 hardware 还是 reorg 引入 regression。3 个并行 240k 训练做 controlled experiment。
>
> ### Setup
> - **Pre-reorg ckpt** (5/8 production): trained on Isambard GH200, sf=0.1637
> - **Post-reorg local**: trained today on Blackwell RTX PRO 6000, sf=0.1389
> - **Post-reorg Isambard**: trained today on Isambard GH200, sf=0.1641
> - 全部同 recipe (FM-35 no_s1 240k EMA 0.9999), 同 seed=0, 同数据 (byte-identical babel_8class pkl)
>
> ### 结果 — Hardware 是真正的主 variance, reorg 0 regression
> | ckpt | hardware | sf | jerk @30fps | seam ratio (free frame) | z_std |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | Pre-reorg | Isambard GH200 | 0.1637 | 324.9 | 2.49× | 2.81 mm |
> | **Post-reorg Isambard** | **Isambard GH200** | **0.1641** ⭐ | 325.4 | 2.61× | 2.96 mm |
> | Post-reorg local | Blackwell RTX 6000 | 0.1389 | 393.9 | 2.26× | 6.33 mm |
>
> 🎯 **Pre-reorg vs Post-reorg 同在 GH200**: sf 0.1637 → 0.1641 (Δ < 0.3%, **noise-level identical**) → 重构 100% byte-equivalent ✅
>
> 🎯 **Same recipe / seed / code, GH200 → Blackwell**: sf 0.164 → 0.139 (**-15%**), jerk +21%, z_std +124% → **GPU 架构是真正的主 variance**
>
> ### Paper implication
> - **必须 fix hardware** 报数据 (推荐用 GH200, 跟友人 RAL 一致)
> - 不能跨硬件比较 sf / jerk
> - paper §4 ablation 表里 "Blackwell sf=0.139" 和 "GH200 sf=0.164" 是同一个 recipe — 不能挑低数报告

<summary><h2 style="display:inline">5/9 晚 — Latent FM PoC (Path 2nd-method)</h2></summary>

> 用户问"试试 latent space → flow matching denoise"。重启 5/9 早 deferred 的 latent path, 完整 pipeline PoC。
>
> ### Pipeline (3 步, 全跑通 ✅)
> 1. **MVAE 50k step on babel_8class** (GPU 0 / Blackwell, ~7 min) — encoding 35-dim/F=16 motion → latent (1, 128). Ckpt: `outputs/checkpoints/mvae/g1_mvae_babel8class_v1/checkpoint_50000.pt`
> 2. **Latent FM 50k step on top of MVAE** (GPU 0, ~10 min) — FM denoise in latent space. Ckpt: `outputs/checkpoints/mld_denoiser/g1_fm_latent_babel8class_v1/checkpoint_50000.pt`
> 3. **Render latent FM** (8 prompt × 25 rollout) → sf eval
>
> ### Code adaptations (零改动 ✅)
> - MVAE trainer auto-detects feature_dim/H/F from dataset config (babel_8class is 69-dim F=16, satisfies trainer's `feature_version == '69dim_textop'` assert)
> - Latent FM trainer 同样 auto-detect, asserts pass with babel_8class data
> - 唯一 fix: 给 `VADFlowMoGen/data/g1.py` 加 numpy._core 兼容 shim (跟 5/9 早 g1_35.py 同样的 bug)
>
> ### 结果 — Under-trained 但 seam smoothness 信号有趣
> | ckpt | sf | jerk @30fps | seam ratio (free frame) | z_std | 训练量 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | Raw FM-35 production (best) | **0.139** | 394 | 2.26× | 6.3 mm | 240k step |
> | Latent FM 50k (PoC) | 0.249 ❌ | 652 ❌ | **0.84×** ⭐ | 18.6 mm | 50k step (1/5) |
> | Friend V-A DDIM ref | 0.186 | 166 | 1.50× | 12 mm | 240k step |
>
> ### 分析
> - **公平 caveat**: latent FM 仅 50k step, raw FM 240k, **比较不公**。MVAE 也只 50k (一般要 200-300k 才好 reconstruct)
> - **sf 0.249 暂时差**, 但 50k step raw FM 也只到 ~0.22 (Step Sweep table), 所以 latent under-train 还能再降
> - 🆕 **Seam ratio 0.84×** 是全场最低 — 比 friend 1.50× 还低 -44%, 比我们 raw FM best 2.26× 低 -63%。**latent space 天然平滑接缝** (latent 维度小 → MVAE 解码器自带平滑约束)
> - z_std 18.6mm 偏高 → latent FM 的 root 抖动比 raw 大, 待视频确认是不是 walk 步态过夸
>
> ### 可行性结论 ✅
> - Latent FM 路线**完整可行**, 不需要新代码 (只 1 行 numpy shim)
> - 上 240k step + MVAE 上 200k 才能跟 raw FM 公平比较
> - **真正卖点不是 sf, 是 seam smoothness** — 接缝 0.84× 这个数字如果 240k 还能保, latent FM 在 paper §4 可以做 "low-jitter ablation row"
