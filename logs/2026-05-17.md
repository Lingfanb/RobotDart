<details open>
<summary><h3 style="display:inline">✅ Butter low-pass on root only</h3></summary>

> **`scripts/butter_smooth_root.py`** + `babel_smooth_sonic_compare.py` 的 smooth_root()
>
> **关键 bug 修复**: v1 用 Euler ZYX 转回 quat 路径 → body 出现 lean artifact (orientation 偏移)
>
> **v2 修法**: 直接 quat smoothing
> ```python
> # Sign-align: 避免 q vs -q 跳变
> for i in range(1, len(rq)):
>     if np.dot(rq[i], rq[i-1]) < 0: rq[i] = -rq[i]
> # Butter 4-component quat
> rq_unnorm = butter_lowpass(rq_aligned, fps, cutoff=3.0)
> # Renormalize
> rq_smooth = rq_unnorm / norm(rq_unnorm)
> ```
>
> **结果** (root-only Butter 3Hz):
> | Clip | orig r_trans | smooth r_trans | reduction |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | 14_F_8 | 675 | **6** | −99% |
> | 14_F_16 | 719 | **18** | −98% |
> | 11_F_12 | 51 | 5 | −90% |
>
> **DOF 不动** → 快动作保留;**root 单独 3Hz 低通** → pelvis 抖动数字上消除
>
> **用户反馈**: 数字大降但视觉仍感觉抖 → 进一步上 SONIC sim physics re-tracking
